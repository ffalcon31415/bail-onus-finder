# Bail Onus as of July 18, 2026

Onus for youth matters is always Crown Onus (YCJA).

For adult matters:

It is crown onus UNLESS any of the following apply (in which case it is reverse onus):

- 524 application is granted - 524(4)
- IPV offence (including sexual offence, crim harass, HT) and the accused has previously been convicted or discharged (with the discharge still on CPIC) for an IPV offence - 515(6)(b.1)
- IPV offence (including ...) and at time of offence was on an IPV peace bond under 810 - 515(6)(b.1) 
- Offence under 145(2) to 145(5) while at large after being released for another offence under the Code - 515(6)(c)
- CDSA offence punishable by imprisonment for life under any of sections 5 to 7, or a conspiracy for such an offence - 515(6)(d)
- Breach of conditional sentence order - 742.6(2)

---

IF the accused is charged with a _straight indictable offence OR a hybrid offence for which Crown has NOT elected summarily_, the following also make it a reverse onus:
- Not ordinarily resident in Canada - 515(6)(b)
- Any assault or sex assault where choking, suffocating, or strangling is alleged - 515(6)(a)(ix)
- The accused was on release for a straight indictable offence or a hybrid offence for which Crown has NOT elected summarily - 515(6)(a)(i)
- Charged with certain firearm possession or other non-use offences (95, 98, 98.1, 99, 100, 102 or 103) - 515(6)(a)(vi)
- Charged with certain offences when committed with a firearm (244, 244.2, 239, 272, 273, 279(1), 279.1, 344 or 346) - 515(6)(a)(vi) to (vii)
- Charged with offences involving a firearm, ammo, prohibited weapon, etc. (see section) while on a prohibition order under the Code (broad definition of prohibition order) - 515(6)(a)(viii) 
- HT offences 279.‍01 or 279.‍011 - 515(6)(a)(x)
- Motor vehicle theft with violence 333.1(3) or for benefit of crim org 333.1(4) - 515(6)(a)(xi)
- Extortion where violence or threats is alleged - 515(6)(a)(xii)
- Violent offence and two or more prior commissions for violent offences (where the Crown did not proceed summarily) - 515(6)(a)(xii.1)
- Residential B&E - 515(6)(a)(xiii) 
- Charged with a violent offence with a weapon with at least 10-year max sentence, and convicted within the past 10 years (previous to the charge date) of an offence in that category where the crown didn't proceed summarily - 515(6)(b.2)  
- Charged with certain crim org offences, terrorism offences, or certain offences under other acts (see the sections) - 515(6)(a)(ii) to (v) and (xiv)
